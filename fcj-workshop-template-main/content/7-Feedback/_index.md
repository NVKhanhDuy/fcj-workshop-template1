### Overall Evaluation

Overall, the internship experience was very meaningful and brought me many valuable lessons. I not only improved my technical knowledge but also learned how to work in a professional environment, communicate effectively, and adapt quickly to new challenges. The program was well organized and gave me the opportunity to learn continuously through both practical tasks and guided activities.

**1. Work Environment**  
The working environment was friendly, open, and highly supportive. I felt comfortable asking questions, sharing ideas, and discussing difficulties because the atmosphere was not too rigid and everyone was willing to help. This made the internship process less stressful and more motivating.

The workplace also had a positive learning culture, where interns were encouraged to explore, ask, and improve themselves step by step. That helped me build confidence during the whole internship period.

**2. Support from Mentors / Admin Team**  
The mentors provided clear explanations and gave me direction when I encountered difficulties. They did not simply provide answers immediately, but also encouraged me to research independently, which helped me develop problem-solving and self-learning skills.

The admin team also played an important role by providing useful information, schedules, and practical guidance. Their support helped me stay on track with the internship plan and complete tasks more smoothly.

**3. Alignment between Work and Major**  
The internship content was very relevant to my academic background and future career orientation. The topics covered were not only theoretical but also connected to real cloud, AI, and technology practice, which made the learning experience more meaningful.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
This internship gave me many opportunities to strengthen both technical and soft skills. On the technical side, I improved my ability to read documentation, analyze systems, write reports, organize worklogs, and understand cloud services in a more structured way.

On the soft skills side, I also developed better time management, teamwork, presentation, and information synthesis skills. In particular, the requirement to work independently, take notes, and organize knowledge made me more proactive and disciplined in learning.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
I appreciated the sharing spirit and team culture throughout the program. People were serious about their work, but at the same time they remained friendly, approachable, and respectful. When problems arose, team members were willing to discuss and find solutions together, which created a very positive learning atmosphere.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
The program was well planned with clear learning objectives, schedules, and practical activities. The workshops, technical sharing sessions, and demo days gave me exposure to many new topics beyond basic knowledge, including AI, DevOps, security, teamwork, and career development.

This was one of the most valuable parts of the internship because it helped me not only complete reports but also broaden my perspective and gain experience from many different angles.

**7. Suggestions for Improvement**  
Although the internship was very positive, there are still some areas that could be improved. For example, adding more hands-on labs and practical exercises would help interns apply theory more effectively. In addition, providing more opportunities for direct interaction with industry professionals and more personalized feedback after each activity could make the learning experience even richer.
