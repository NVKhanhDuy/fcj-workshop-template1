# Summary Report: "FCAJ Community Day (June 13)"
### Purpose of the Event
The FCAJ Community Day (June 13) event was organized to share practical knowledge, real-world experience, and technology trends in the field of information technology. It helped participants better understand the working environment in large organizations, career opportunities, the applications of AI and cloud computing, and the direction for future learning and professional development.

### Speakers and Topics
* **Dat and Cuong** - Topic: Real stories and culture in multinational corporations
* **Hoang Trong** - Topic: DevOps Engineer
* **Hieu Nghi** - Topic: From the first cloud AI exploration journey to becoming an AWS partner
* **Kien and Tho** - Topic: From the first cloud AI exploration journey to becoming an AWS partner

### Notable Content of the Presentations

#### 1. Real stories and culture in multinational corporations - Speaker: Dat and Cuong
The session shared real experiences from working in multinational companies and explained how organizational culture affects work style, communication, collaboration, and career development.

#### 2. DevOps Engineer - Speaker: Hoang Trong
The talk introduced the role of a DevOps Engineer, the software development and operations process, commonly used tools such as Git, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, and CI/CD, and the career opportunities in the DevOps field.

#### 3. From the first cloud AI exploration journey to becoming an AWS partner - Speaker: Hieu Nghi
The speaker shared the journey of learning and applying AI on cloud platforms, the AI services offered by AWS, and the learning and development path to become an AWS partner.

#### 4. From the first cloud AI exploration journey to becoming an AWS partner - Speaker: Kien and Tho
The session introduced the experience of implementing AI solutions on AWS, the practical lessons learned during project development, how to leverage AWS services, and the steps needed to grow toward becoming an AWS partner.

### What I Learned

* A clearer understanding of the working environment in multinational organizations, including corporate culture, communication skills, teamwork, and adaptability to international environments.
* Insight into the role of a DevOps Engineer, modern software development and operations processes, and widely used tools and technologies such as Git, Docker, Kubernetes, and CI/CD.
* Better understanding of AI and cloud computing on the AWS platform, how AI services are applied in practice, and the future direction of cloud technology.
* A clearer view of learning and career development paths in DevOps, AI, and Cloud through the speakers' experiences.
* Awareness of the importance of continuous learning, improving technical and soft skills, and obtaining international certifications to strengthen competitiveness in the IT industry.
* Additional motivation and clearer career direction, helping me identify the knowledge and skills needed to meet future business requirements.

### Key Lessons Learned
The event not only provided technical knowledge but also offered practical perspectives on the working environment and current technology trends. Through this experience, I gained a better understanding of career opportunities in the IT field and developed a stronger direction for planning my own learning and personal growth in the future.
